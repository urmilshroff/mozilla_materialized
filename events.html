GameScript

A simple snake game using JavaScript

N.O.W.'s first event organised by a team of 5 people, GameScript has now moved on to be the flagship event people expect from us. We taught people how to make the classic game of Snake in JavaScript using just 100 lines of code!

Android App Competition

Shedding light on the most skilled app developers

N.O.W. collaborated with the Department of Computer Engineering to organize the Android App Competition in April. With an audience of over 200 students, our skills were put to the test as this was the first major event that N.O.W. was a part of.

GitHack

A crash course on Git and GitHub

A quick and simple explanation on how people can contribute to Open Source projects on GitHub and learn the basics of how the Git VCS works. This was in collaboration with Hacktoberfest, a month-long celebration of the Open Web culture with challenging events and exciting prizes for participants.

Savishkar

An Annual Competition for final year Computer Engineering

savishkar is an Annual Competition for final year Computer Engineering to showcase their projects and compete by Computer Engineering Department in collaboration with N.O.W.
