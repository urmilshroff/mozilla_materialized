GameScript

A simple Snake game using JavaScript

N.O.W.'s first event organised by a team of 5 people, GameScript has moved on to becoming the flagship event people expect from us. We taught people how to make the classic game of Snake in JavaScript using just 100 lines of code!

Android App Competition

Shedding light on the most skilled App Developers

N.O.W. collaborated with the Department of Computer Engineering to organize the Android App Competition in April. With an audience of over 200 students, our skills were put to the test as this was the first major event that N.O.W. was a part of.

Savishkar

Competing for the best Final Year Projects

Savishkar is an annual competition hosted by N.O.W. in collaboration with the Computer Engineering Department of our college. It is a place for F.Y. Computer Engineering students to showcase their F.Y.P.s and compete for the best and most innovative ones.

GitHack

A crash course on Git and GitHub

A quick and simple workshop on how people can contribute to Open Source projects on GitHub and learn the basics of how the Git VCS works. This was in collaboration with Hacktoberfest, a month-long celebration of the Open Web culture with challenging events and exciting prizes for participants.
